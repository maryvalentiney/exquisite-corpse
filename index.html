Deep in the forest there is a small pool in a clearing. The pool glows softly with an eerie light and you can see a small figure kneeling at it's edge. The figure is a mushroom fairy! She has a small hat made from a mushroom cap and gauzy delicate wings. She is singing to the pool and we can see the water start to shift and morph. The water is rolling and bubbling now, but there is no sign of a creature breaking the surface. It is almost like the water itself is alive.
